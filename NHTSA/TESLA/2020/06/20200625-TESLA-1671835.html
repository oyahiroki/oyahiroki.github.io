WHEN IT HAPPENS VARIES ON MANY FACTORS, BUT WILL HAPPEN TYPICALLY BEFORE 100,000. WHEN THE MCU1 FAILS, THE CAR CANNOT BE OPERATED. ADDITIONALLY , THE MCU2 UPGRADE REMOVES RADIO FEATURES THAT WERE AVAILABLE ON MCU1. THE REASON FOR THIS IS THAT TESLA DOES NOT WANT TO OFFER AN ADAPTER CABLE AND NEW TUNER UNIT. MCU1 CAME WITH AN ANALOG RADIO. MCU2 WORKS WITH THEIR NEWER DIGITAL RADIO.
